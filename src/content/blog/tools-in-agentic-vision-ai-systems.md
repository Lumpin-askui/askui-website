## FAQ

### How do Vision AI Agents improve the accuracy of sports analysis?

Vision AI Agents leverage state-of-the-art pre-trained models and continuously adapt to new advancements in computer vision. This ensures the system uses the most accurate tools available, significantly improving the reliability of the analysis.

### What are the main benefits of using pre-trained models in sports analysis?

Pre-trained models reduce development time and improve accuracy by providing a strong foundation that has already been trained on large datasets. This allows developers to focus on fine-tuning the models for specific sports-related tasks, rather than building everything from scratch.

### Can Vision AI Agents adapt to new models and tools as they emerge?

Yes, Vision AI Agents are designed to be flexible and adaptable. They can easily integrate new models and tools, ensuring that the system remains current and effective in the face of evolving technology.

### What type of sports analysis can be performed using Vision AI Agents?

Vision AI Agents can be used for a wide range of sports analysis tasks, including object detection (identifying players and equipment), pose estimation (analyzing body positions), and action recognition (detecting specific movements or events).
