## FAQ

### What is the primary benefit of having excellent customer support for test automation?

Excellent customer support significantly increases the adoption rate of new automation tools and helps teams overcome resistance to change, saving time and resources by quickly resolving issues.

### Why is human support preferred over chatbots or documentation?

For complex issues in test automation, human support offers personalized, real-time assistance that chatbots and documentation often cannot provide. This direct interaction can lead to faster resolutions and a better understanding of the tool.

### How does tailored onboarding contribute to successful test automation adoption?

Customized onboarding programs provide step-by-step guidance specific to your product and tech stack, increasing user satisfaction and ensuring a smoother implementation process. This focused approach helps teams quickly grasp the tool's capabilities and integrate it effectively into their workflows.

### What should I look for in an automation partner to ensure strong support?

Prioritize partners who offer real-time human support, a clear onboarding roadmap, a willingness to address your unique challenges, and evidence of success with companies similar to yours. This ensures comprehensive and personalized assistance throughout your test automation journey.

### How does ongoing support contribute to the long-term success of test automation?

Continuous support helps teams adapt to changes in the CI/CD flow and maintain efficiency. By investing in ongoing training and support, companies can reduce testing time and ensure that their automation tools continue to deliver value as their environment evolves.
