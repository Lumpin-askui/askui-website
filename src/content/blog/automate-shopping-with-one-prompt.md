## From Prompt to Cart: How the Demo Works

Here is the prompt I used for Caesr testing:

> **Example prompt**  
> "Open Chrome, go to Amazon search for foot hammock, and add one marked as Amazon's overall pick to my basket."

Caesr turned the complete workflow from single prompt.

1. **Open Chrome** - started with new tab.
2. **Navigate to the store** - went directly to Amazon.
3. **Search for the Product that I want** - typed "foot hammock" in the search bar.
4. **Choose the item** - selected the product marked as Amazon's "Overall Pick".
5. **Add to my basket** - by adding to my cart, finished the task.
