## One cannot overlook the pivotal role of prompting in effectively deploying agentic AI systems. The success of these systems hinges on clear, detailed prompts that provide the AI agent with context and guidance. Poorly-conceived prompts can lead to misunderstandings and erroneous outputs, underscoring the necessity for prompt precision.

## Agentic AI boasts several advantages. Its modularity and adaptability lend it an edge in the dynamic realm of computer vision. By choosing and combining various tools and models, agentic AI remains versatile and up-to-date with the latest advancements in technology. Further, when confronting complex tasks, such as those requiring multiple steps, agentic AI excels due to its ability to deconstruct problems and leverage an assortment of tools. This iterative refinement leads to improved accuracy and enables efficient data processing at scales beneficial for organizations with vast image and video collections.
